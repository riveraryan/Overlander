Stop Dreaming. Start Doing.

Overlanding is vehicle based adventure travel that combines elements of camping and off-roading. It pits you and your vehicle against the elements. Whether its as intense as dense forest, river-crossings, and high mountain passes or simply a trip to the beach, proper planning is key. Our suite of tools will help have a successful trip.

Plan Your Next Adventure.

Build, modify, save, and access your trip from anywhere using our intuitive web app. Set times, dates, locations, packing lists, vehicle requirements, and more.

Coordinate With Friends

Overlander allows you to connect with other overlanding enthusiasts in your area. Connect with new friends and invite them to join your event. Manage trip participants. Form a convoy.

Discover The Outdoors.

Don't let any missed details get in the way of your adventure. Overlander helps you organize your trip so that you are prepared.
